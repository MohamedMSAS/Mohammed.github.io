    Home Budgie Gallery Budgie Facts
  1. Budgies live upto 20 years and weigh about 30 to 40 grams
  1. When budgies feel threatened, they fly upto the highest perch available and bring their feathers together to appear thinner and smaller
  1. Male budgies are considered to be one of the top five talking champions amongst parrot species
  1. Budgies hearing range goes upto 400 - 20,000HZ and can mimic sounds
  1. Budgies have a very high resting respiration rate
